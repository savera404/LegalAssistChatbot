**Case Name:**  
MIRZA SHAHID BAIG v. MST. LUBNA RIAZ AND 2 OTHERS  

**Court:**  
Lahore High Court  

**Year:**  
2004  

**Legal Issue:**  
Whether the petitioner is entitled to recover dowry articles or the amount of Rs.3,50,000, and whether the suit is barred by law (including issues of estoppel, admissibility of evidence, and statutory limits on dowry).  

**Relevant Law / Sections:**  
- West Pakistan Family Courts Act (XXXV of 1964), Sections 5 and 17  
- Constitution of Pakistan (1973), Article 199 (Interpretation of Statutes)  
- Qanun-e-Shahadat Order, 1984  
- Bridal Gift Restriction Act, 1976, Section 3  
- Contract Act, Section 23  

**Key Facts (brief):**  
- Marriage solemnised 26‑10‑1997; minor son born 15‑12‑1998.  
- Divorce granted by Family Court 20‑10‑1999.  
- Respondent filed suit for recovery of dowry articles (or Rs.3,50,000) on 09‑12‑1999.  
- Trial Court awarded Rs.2,88,000; appellate court modified to Rs.2,50,000.  
- Petitioner filed a writ petition challenging the lower courts’ decisions.
