**Case Name:**  
Mian Fazl‑e‑Kareem and others v. Khan Muhammad and others  

**Court:**  
Peshawar High Court (Civil Revision No. 556‑P of 2016)  

**Year:**  
2023 (judgment dated 19 October 2023)  

**Legal Issue:**  
Whether the entry of Bibi Hawa as owner of one‑quarter of the property in the revenue record is illegal and ineffective, and whether the petitioners are entitled to claim the remaining fifteen‑sixteenths of the property.  

**Relevant Law / Sections:**  
- Qanun‑e‑Shahadat (10 of 1984)  
- Khyber Pakhtunkhwa (NWFP) Muslim Personal Law (Shariat) Application Act, 1935  
- West Pakistan Muslim Personal Law (Shariat) Application Act, 1962  
- Supreme Court decisions:  
  - Maqbool Ahmad v. Fazal‑i‑Haq (2012 SCMR 917)  
  - Additional Settlement Commissioner (Land) Sargoda v. Muhammad Shafi (1971 SC 791)  
- Punjab Muslim Personal Law (Shariat) Application (Removal of Difficulties) Act, 1975
